Guerilla Marketing

Guerrilla marketing is all about bold, unconventional strategies that surprise, engage, and leave a lasting impression. It relies on creativity, timing, and relevance — not massive budgets — to cut through the noise and connect with audiences on a deeper, more memorable level.


At Communications Arc, we leverage guerrilla tactics to help brands spark conversations, generate buzz, and create viral moments in unexpected ways. Whether it’s through experiential activations, street-level interactions, or creative stunts, we craft experiences that align with brand values while driving impact in real time.

What We Provide

At Communications Arc, we provide end-to-end guerrilla marketing services that include concept development, location scouting, audience targeting, on-ground activation management, experiential installations, influencer and digital amplification, as well as post-campaign buzz monitoring. We take a strategic and insight-led approach to ensure each campaign is not only creatively bold but also aligned with brand values and audience expectations. From the initial spark of an idea to full-scale execution, we handle all logistics, permitting, staffing, and content production, ensuring that every moment is crafted for real-time impact and long-term brand recall. Our team also works to extend campaign reach beyond the physical space by leveraging influencer partnerships, media engagement, and viral storytelling opportunities — turning one-time stunts into sustained conversations and measurable results.

The Challenge

In an oversaturated market, traditional advertising was no longer delivering the breakthrough moments the client needed. The brand was looking for a way to reconnect with its audience in a more human, memorable, and unexpected way — without relying on high media spends.


They needed a campaign that would spark conversation, generate organic reach, and create emotional resonance — but without the risk of being seen as gimmicky or off-brand. The biggest challenge was balancing boldness with authenticity while maintaining full control over execution and outcomes.
